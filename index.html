Our Story

Meet the Man Behind the Mission

From a celebrated journalist to a changemaker for tribal communities

Biswajit Jha — "Book Dada"

Biswajit Jha is a social entrepreneur and educator who left a successful career as a sports editor at Zee News in Delhi to uplift underprivileged children in North Bengal.

Together with his wife Dr. Sanjukta Saha, he founded the Rajganj Maa Sarada Shishu Tirtha Charitable Educational Society in Mendabari — running free schools, coaching centers, and a football academy for tribal children.

His work has transformed over a thousand lives, proving that one individual's commitment can ignite a community's future.

Our Impact

Creating Lasting Change

Real transformation happening in tribal communities of North Bengal

Educational Uplift

Measurable improvements in enrollment, retention, and academic performance among tribal children.

Sports Empowerment

Football academy alumni achieving professional success and competing at state levels.

Cultural Revival

Students gaining confidence in their tribal identity through art, music, dance, and language.

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide with computer labs and AI education in remote villages.

Girls' Empowerment

Declining early marriages and increasing female education participation rates.

Community Trust

Parents actively supporting education, with local women becoming teachers and role models.
